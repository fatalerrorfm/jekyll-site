About Fatal Error

“I bet we can talk for thirty minutes about that.”

Fatal Error is a podcast about software development, hosted by Soroush Khanlou and Chris Dzombak. We discuss topics at the cutting edge of software architecture, Swift and iOS development, and software development best practices.

Even-numbered episodes are available exclusively to our Patreon supporters, who we thank for their support.

Fatal Error ended after its fourth season.
